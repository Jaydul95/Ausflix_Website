Our Story

LEADING THE WAY IN MEDIA
PRODUCTIONS

AusFlix is an Australian Digital Media company with a strong international focus. The company thrives on its creative team's talent and commitment to develop affordable, high-quality media productions targeted to an international audience.

AusFlix productions leads the way in developing tele-drama series adored by its ever-increasing fan base. Tele-Drama and other content is streamed to a captured audience on AusFlix's own online streaming service, CeyFLiX™.

AusFlix works with top TV and media organisations to forge strategic partnerships, cross-promote content, and secure targeted advertising opportunities that expand reach across borders.
